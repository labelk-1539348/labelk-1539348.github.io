About Me

I am from Sammamish, Washington and have lived there my whole life. I have two older brothers named Ian and Jordan and a boy cat named Lulu. My family moved to Washington from Hawaii before I was born but I still have a lot of family members that live in Hawaii and visit them every other year. I am visiting them this Christmas.

I spent most of my time dancing competitively at Dance Premier. I did ballet, jazz, lyrical, contemporary, hiphop, and tap. My favorite style is ballet and contemporary. I enjoyed ballet because it was very structural and easy to see improvement because there's only one correct way to do most moves. I also liked contemporary because it is very expressive and there's always new moves that you can come up with.
